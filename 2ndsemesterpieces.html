Gelli Printing Collage - Featured

Closer Look

Throughout my 2nd Semester of Studio Art 1, my favorite piece would be my Gelli Printing Collage. I believe this piece captures my art skills and creativity, and shows a little bit about me through art. I wanted to combine the idea of music and art, two of my favorite hobbies, into one piece. This piece represents how sometimes it all feels like too much. There have been times when I've wanted to quit a hobby that means so much to me. The pressure from others and competition makes it hard to stay motivated, and leads to disinterest in the subject. Over time, I have learned to love art by following my own rules and understanding how to enjoy it. This piece gave me a chance to show my love for the hobbies that I love, using a technique that I have never used before.
